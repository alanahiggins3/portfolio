NASA DEVLEOP Belize Water Resources

Jet Propulsion Laboratory, Pasadena CA June 2019 to August 2019 For my second term with the NASA DEVELOP program, I was part of a Water Resources team. Over ten weeks, my team and I created a Google Earth Engine dashboard to analyze water quality parameters of the Belize Barrier Reef Reserve System in Central America. The two parameters we studied were turbidity (water clarity) and chlorophyll-a (water color) concentrations, which are characterisitcs used as proxy data to determine water and reef health.

Utilizing Data Driven Pages for City Zoning

California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo February 2018 Urbanized areas are often difficult to represent spatially as cities have high population densities and landuse can change frequently. Data Driven Pages is a useful tool in the ArcMap toolbox that allows users to break up the area of their city page by page. Each page represents one square unit of an entire city grid. By including an inset map, users can gauge where they are within the city for better context.
